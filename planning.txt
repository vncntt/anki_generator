chome extension that saves articles you want to remember into anki decks that you can download



workflow:

click on the extension icon if you want to save this website
-> 
extract text from the article through readability.js
->
send the text of article + a prompt to gemini telling it to create anki cards for this website
-> 
have a downloadable .apkg file with ~3-20 anki cards from the content on the website
